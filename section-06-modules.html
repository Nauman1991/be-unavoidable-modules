Eight Curated Modules

What You Actually Learn

This isn't theory. This is survival. Here's exactly what you'll master.

This is not a program that teaches you to sell random products or services. It is a program that teaches you to sell the most valuable thing you own: you.

Module 1

No One Is Coming For You

The world changed. Your playbook didn't. We start with the data — not motivation, not platitudes. You'll understand exactly what's happening, why the passive approach is now a risk, and what your actual options are.

Module 2

How People Actually Make Decisions

People don't buy with logic. They buy with emotion and justify with logic after. You'll learn the psychology of decision-making — how people think, feel, and behave when choosing who to hire, who to keep, and who to let go.

Module 3

Pain vs. Pleasure

Benefits don't move people. Consequences do. You'll learn why pain is a more powerful motivator than pleasure — and how to make the cost and consequence of not having you unmistakably clear.

Module 4

What Happens If They Don't Choose You

The cost of absence. You'll learn how to quantify what a problem is actually costing someone — in dollars, in time, in opportunity, in frustration. This is where vague value becomes undeniable business impact.

Module 5

Find the Person Who Can Say Yes

Not everyone has the power to choose you. You'll learn how to identify who actually controls your future — and how to get in front of them before decisions are made.

Module 6

Become an Expert Diagnostician

The best salespeople aren't pitching. They're diagnosing. You'll learn how to ask questions that uncover what people actually need versus what they say they need — and position yourself as the solution.

Module 7

Know When You're in the Right Place

Not every opportunity is worth your time. You'll learn how to qualify opportunities against a clear set of criteria — so you stop wasting energy on roles, projects, or people that were never going to choose you anyway.

Module 8

Gain Commitment and Close

You've built trust. You've uncovered the problem. You've quantified the cost. Now you need to close. You'll learn how to facilitate a decision process that puts your value front and center — whether you're negotiating a salary, asking for a promotion, or landing a deal.

Who Built This

Curated by sales veterans who've carried a bag, managed teams, and built sales organizations. Not academics. Not coaches. People who've done it.
