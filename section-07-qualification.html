Honest Qualification

This Isn't For Everyone

We'd be the first to tell you if this isn't the right fit. That counterintuitive honesty is classic good selling.

You're a Good Fit If:

  • You've been displaced by AI or fear imminent job loss
  • You can't afford 5 years of school or don't want to start your career over
  • You're willing to learn skills that feel uncomfortable at first
  • You recognize the passive approach ("do good work and hope someone notices") is now a risk
  • You want a clear, actionable path forward — not motivational content

You're Not a Good Fit If:

  • You're looking for motivational content or inspirational speeches
  • You think your job is safe because you "do good work"
  • You want to learn to prompt AI better
  • You're not willing to practice selling yourself
  • You believe sales skills are manipulative or beneath you

That counterintuitive honesty — qualifying the buyer — is classic good selling. It increases trust with the people it's for, because they feel guided rather than pitched.
